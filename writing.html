File A/02 / Biography

About

Verified
record

I am a researcher trained in international relations, working at the intersection of institutional accountability, information rights and Africa China relations.

My work starts from records. Diplomatic chronologies, disclosure requests, procurement trails and open sources in multiple languages. I am interested in what institutions write down, what they withhold, and what the gap between the two reveals about how power actually operates.

I hold a BA in International Relations from Stellenbosch University and have worked as a research assistant on Russia Ukraine diplomatic history, as an administrator in the non-profit sector, and as an independent litigant in UK information rights proceedings.

File C/01 / Venture

Bridge
Africa China

Founding
document

A consulting and intelligence platform connecting African and Chinese institutions across policy, trade and diplomacy.

The premise is simple. The Africa China relationship is the most consequential economic corridor of the coming decades, and it is chronically under-analysed from the African side. Bridge Africa China exists to close that gap with rigorous, records-based intelligence rather than sentiment in either direction.
